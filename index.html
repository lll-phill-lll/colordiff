About:

  • Presets 1 and 2 illustrate how the Euclidean distance method inaccurately identifies the closest pair of colors.
  • 3 preset shows that both methods struggle to accurately identify similar colors.
  • Random button selects three random colors and calculates the metrics for them.
  • Smart random button selects three colors where the Euclidean metric clearly fails from a human perspective.
